What's inside

Everything you need
to go further.

Organized around the actual curriculum, not hashtags or algorithms.

01

Test Levels

The complete, up to date requirements for every track, from Learn to Skate USA through U.S. Figure Skating senior level. Flip between quick reference and full descriptions with technique diagrams. No more digging through PDFs.

02

Video Examples

Test requirements link straight to real skater videos tagged to that exact skill. See an eight step sequence or a twizzle done for real.

03

Elements Library

Search and filter the full clip library by element, including edges, turns, jumps, spins, and 70+ named skills. A fast visual feed built for how skaters actually study.

04

Your Clips, Your Progress

Upload your own videos, tag the elements they contain, and keep them in one place. And next time you want to show off your progress, just search the element name and see it all.
